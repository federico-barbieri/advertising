THE BRIEF

Minute Maid had released their global tagline Kindness is the secret and their local branch requested a campaign to highlight this new tagline for the local market.

THE IDEA

We came up with Mother Language, a bond between moms and children that only they can understand. The secret behind it? You guessed it: kindness.
